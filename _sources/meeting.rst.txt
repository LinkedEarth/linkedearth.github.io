Meeting Schedule and Notes
==========================

Community Meeting
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

LinkedEarth holds biweekly community meetings on Mondays 9am Pacific. See our `calendar <http://linked.earth/calendar.html>`_ for details.

Development Meeting
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Development meetings for `Pyleoclim <http://linked.earth/analysis.html>`_ are help biweekly on Tuesdays 1pm Pacific. See our `calendar <http://linked.earth/calendar.html>`_ for details.
