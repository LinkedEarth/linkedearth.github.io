Contact LinkedEarth
===================

**SLACK**

Announcements and general discussion is done through our Slack workspace. `Email us <mailto:linkedearth@gmail.com>`_ for an invitation. This is the best place to start getting involved with the community.


**GITHUB**

All technical discussion should go through GitHub. Please use project-specific GitHub repository.

**OTHER MEDIA**

LinkedEarth's online presence also includes:

* Medium Blog: `https://linkedearth.medium.com/about <https://linkedearth.medium.com/about>`_
* Twitter: `https://twitter.com/linked_earth <https://twitter.com/linked_earth>`_

If you wish to contact LinkedEarth via a non-public channel, email us at `linkedearth@gmail.com <mailto:linkedearth@gmail.com>`_.
