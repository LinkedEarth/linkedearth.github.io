.. _funders:

Funders and Collaborators
=========================

National Science Foundation
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

LinkedEarth has originated from the `NSF EarthCube program <https://earthcube.org/>`_. This award supported the creation of the :ref:`PaCTS standard <data_stewards>`, the :ref:`LinkedEarth wiki <https://wiki.linked.earth/Main_Page>`, and :ref:`Pyleoclim <analysis>`.

:ref:`GeoChronR <analysis>` was supported by a grant for the `NSF Geoinformatics program <https://www.nsf.gov/funding/pgm_summ.jsp?pims_id=503447>`_.

:ref:`PReSto <data_products>` is funded under the `NSF Geoinformatics program <https://www.nsf.gov/funding/pgm_summ.jsp?pims_id=503447>`_.

The `NSF EarthCube program <https://earthcube.org/>`_ is currently supporting most of the :ref:`analysis` and :ref:`community` activities under the `PaleoCube award <https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2126510&HistoricalAwards=false>`_.

NOAA
^^^^

:ref:`The Last Millennium Reanalysis <data_products>` was developed under a NOAA grant.

Industry
^^^^^^^^

:ref:`Pyleoclim <analysis>` was further supported by a grant from `JP Morgan AI Research <https://www.jpmorgan.com/insights/technology/artificial-intelligence>`_, supporting the development of :ref:`autoTS <analysis>`.

Office of Naval Research
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

:ref:`ClimateDISK <analysis>` is supported by a grant from the Office of Naval Research to Gil.
