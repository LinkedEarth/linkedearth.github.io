Data Products¶

Climate Reconstructions¶

The LinkedEarth team has been involved in the Last Millennium Reanalysis (LMR). Version 2 of the dataset is available from NOAA.

The LinkedEarth team has also been funded through a NSF Geoinformatics grant to develop a Paleoclimate Reconstruction Storehouse (PReSto). The goal is to provide broad access to paleoclimate reconstructions on the internet.

Partnership¶

The team has been involved in various projects with PAGES:

  • Pages2k

  • Iso2k

  • CoralHydro2k

  • Temp12k.

As well as with the German-funded PALMOD project.

The data for these projects are available in LiPD format through the LiPDverse.
